Game of the Year 2017

"The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" won the Game of the Year (GOTY) award in 2017 at The Game Awards. Competing against titles like 'Super Mario Odyssey', 'Persona 5', and 'Horizon Zero Dawn', BOTW was praised for its breathtaking open-world design, innovative mechanics, and engaging storytelling. This win solidified Nintendo's legacy in the gaming industry, proving that "BOTW redefined the open-world genre". Even years later, it remains one of the most celebrated video games of all time.
