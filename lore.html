Lore

The Great Calamity

10,000 years ago, the people of Hyrule built powerful machines called the Divine Beasts and the Guardians to aid in their battle against the evil entity, Calamity Ganon. With the help of these creations and a heroic warrior, they sealed Ganon away.

The Fall of Hyrule

100 years before the events of Breath of the Wild, Calamity Ganon returned, taking control of the Divine Beasts and Guardians. The Champions—Mipha, Daruk, Revali, and Urbosa—were defeated, and Hyrule fell into ruin. Princess Zelda used her divine power to trap Ganon within Hyrule Castle, buying time for the hero, Link, to recover.

Link’s Awakening

After being placed in the Shrine of Resurrection, Link awakens with no memories of his past. Guided by the mysterious voice of Zelda, he sets out on a journey to reclaim the Divine Beasts, defeat Ganon, and restore peace to Hyrule.

The Legacy of Zelda

Throughout the ages, Zelda has played a crucial role in protecting Hyrule. In BOTW, she struggles with her own destiny, seeking to unlock her divine powers while burdened by the expectations of her lineage. Her courage and sacrifice shape the fate of the kingdom.
