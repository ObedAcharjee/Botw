Characters

Link

The hero of Hyrule, chosen to defeat Calamity Ganon. Link is the main, playable character in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. He is also the main character in all other Zelda games, and in Breath of the Wild, he wakes up after a 100 year sleep and ventures to save Hyrule from Calamity Ganon. He regains some of his memories along the way, revealing that he was friends with Princess Zelda. Link is 17 years old.

Princess Zelda

The princess of Hyrule, burdened with the duty of sealing away Calamity Ganon. Zelda plays a crucial role in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Unlike past incarnations, she struggles with unlocking her divine power, feeling the weight of her destiny. Throughout the game, Zelda’s memories reveal her dedication to research, her complex relationship with Link, and her deep desire to protect Hyrule. After sealing Calamity Ganon for 100 years, she awaits Link’s return to finally restore peace to the kingdom. Zelda is 17 years old.

Daruk

The mighty Goron Champion, sworn to protect Hyrule. Daruk is one of the four Champions in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, chosen to pilot the Divine Beast Vah Rudania. A powerful warrior with an unbreakable spirit, he is known for his immense strength and unwavering loyalty to his friends. Daruk possesses the unique ability Daruk’s Protection, a defensive shield that deflects all attacks. Though fearless in battle, he has an amusing fear of dogs. Even after his passing, his spirit aids Link in the fight against Calamity Ganon.

Mipha

The gentle and kindhearted Zora Champion, gifted with the power of healing. Mipha was chosen to pilot the Divine Beast Vah Ruta in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. She possessed a deep bond with Link, having crafted the Zora Armor specifically for him as a symbol of her feelings. Mipha’s ability, Mipha’s Grace, restores life to those on the brink of death, a testament to her caring nature. Though she fell in battle against Waterblight Ganon, her spirit remains, guiding Link and aiding him in his quest to save Hyrule.

Revali

The proud and skilled Rito Champion, master of the skies. Revali was chosen to pilot the Divine Beast Vah Medoh in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. A highly talented archer, he possessed unmatched aerial agility and developed Revali’s Gale, a powerful updraft ability that allows for swift movement through the air. Though confident—sometimes to the point of arrogance—Revali’s pride masked a deep determination to protect Hyrule. Despite his rivalry with Link, he ultimately recognized his strength. Even after his fall to Windblight Ganon, his spirit aids Link in the battle against Calamity Ganon.

Urbosa

The fierce and noble Gerudo Champion, protector of Hyrule’s desert kingdom. Urbosa was chosen to pilot the Divine Beast Vah Naboris in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. As the chief of the Gerudo, she was a powerful warrior and a wise leader, respected by her people. She wielded Urbosa’s Fury, a devastating lightning-based ability capable of striking down foes instantly. Urbosa shared a close bond with Princess Zelda, acting as both a mentor and protector. Though she fell in battle against Thunderblight Ganon, her spirit continues to aid Link in the fight to defeat Calamity Ganon.
